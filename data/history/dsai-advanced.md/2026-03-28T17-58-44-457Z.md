### $l_1/l_2$ Regularization

Modell einschränken, damit Parameter $\theta$ klein bleiben
<!-- .element: class="fragment fade-in" -->
$$cost_{reg} = cost + \lambda\cdot reg$$
<!-- .element: class="fragment fade-in" -->

$$reg_{l1} = \sum|{\theta}|$$
<!-- .element: class="fragment fade-in" -->
$$reg_{l2} = \sum\theta^2$$
<!-- .element: class="fragment fade-in" -->
